Chorizo Carbonara

  • 15 minutes
  • Serves 4
  • Ingredients
  • Method
  • Spaghetti
    400g
  • Chorizo
    100g
  • Peppers
    2
  • Eggs
    4
  • Parmesan
    75g
  • Smoked Paprika
    1/2 tsp
  • Extra Virign Olive Oil
    1 tbsp
  1. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to boil and add spaghetti. Cook spaghetti for 8 mins or so, until al dente.
  2. Remove skin from chorizo, then break it up into small chunks using your hands. Heat olive oil in pan, then add chorizo. Get the chorizo sizzling, cook until its start to turn a darker colour and crispy. Remove and set aside approx 2 tbsp of cooked chorizo, this is to top pasta when serving.
  3. In a separate small bowl, mix together egg yolks, parmesan and black pepper unto combined.
  4. Reserve a decent amount of pasta water before transferring cooked spaghetti directly to pan with chorizo. Add a ladle of pasta water to pan and and toss spaghetti and chorizo together until combined.
  5. Add 2 tbsp of pasta water to eggy-parmesan mixture and mix together before adding mixture to spaghetti, along with smoked paprika and chilli flakes. Remove pan from heat and toss until eggy-parmesan mixture is combined with pasta. Add more pasta water if you wish to make it more glossy.
  6. Finally, add a squeeze of lemon, lemon zest and season to taste before serving. Best served immediately.
