Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 30 minutes
baking
  • Ingredients
  • Method
  • Gluten Free Self raising flour
    225g
  • Brown sugar
    170g
  • Butter
    140g
  • Dark chocolate
    100g
  • Eggs
    2
  • Cinnamon
    1 tsp
  • Nutmeg
    1/2 tsp
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C/360°F/160°C Fan
  2. Break the chocolate up into bits
  3. Mix butter and sugar
  4. Slowly add eggs and mix
  5. Mix in flour and the chocolate chips
  6. Split the mix into 20-25 individual cookies on a baking tray
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
