Jambalaya

  • 40 minutes
  • Serves 4
meal
  • Ingredients
  • Method
  • Chicken breast
    300g
  • Peppers
    2
  • Onion
    1
  • Celery sticks
    2
  • Garlic cloves
    2
  • Cajun seasoning
    2 tbsp
  • Smoked Paprika
    1 tsp
  • Dried Oregano
    1/2 tsp
  • Worcestershire Sauce
    1 tbsp
  • Rice
    200g
  • Chopped tomatoes
    1 tin
  • Chorizo
    150g
  • Chicken Stock
    400 ml
  1. Dice the chicken and the chorizo.
  2. Dice the peppers, onion and celery sticks. Peel the garlic and mince / grate.
  3. Spray a large, shallow casserole dish with low-calorie cooking spray, add the chicken and cook over a medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the peppers, onion and celery and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the garlic, cajun spice, paprika and oregano, and cook for a further minute, to release the flavours.
  6. Add the rice, chopped tomatoes, stock and Worcestershire sauce. Stir in the chorizo and bring to the boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low, cover and cook for 25 minutes or until all the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is tender.
