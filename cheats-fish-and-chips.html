Cheat's Fish and Chips

  • 45 minutes
  • Serves 2
meal
  • Ingredients
  • Method
  • White fish
    250g
  • Potatoes
    400g
  • Streaky bacon rasher
    1
  • Stale bread
    60g
  • Plain flour
    30g
  • Large egg
    1
  • Frozen peas
    250g
  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C/200°C Fan.
  2. Scrub the potatoes, chop lengthways into 2cm wedges, toss with 1 tbsp of olive oil and a pich of sea salt and black pepper, then arrange in a single layer on a roasting tray.
  3. Roast for 40 minutes, or until golden and cooked through, shaking halfway.
  4. Meanwhile, roughly chop the bacon and place in a food processor, tear in the bread, add 1 tbsp of oil and blitz into fine crumbs, then spread across a plate.
  5. Spread the flour across a second plate and beat the egg on another.
  6. One by one, turn the fish fish in the flour, then the egg, letting any excess drip off, and finally turn in the bacon crumb.
  7. Line up on a baking tray and roast for 15 minutes, or until golden.
  8. Cook the peas in a pan of boiling water and serve with the fish and chips!
